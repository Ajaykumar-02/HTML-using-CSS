Delicious Paneer Butter Masala

A creamy and flavorful Indian dish made with cottage chesse and rich tomato gravy

Ingredients


  • 250g Paneer (Cottage Cheese)
  • 2 medium-sized onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 10 cashews, soaked
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • Crushed kasuri methi

Steps to Prepare


  1. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and cashews with spices.
  2. Blend into a silky puree.
  3. Simmer with butter and whole spices.
  4. Add paneer, crushed kasuri methi, and finish with cream.

Nutrition Facts


Nutrient Amount (per serving)
Calories 400 kcal
Protein 15g
Fat 30g
Carbohydrates 15g
