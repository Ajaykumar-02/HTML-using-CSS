⚲ kedarnath temple Camp Trek


Kedarnath Temple, located in the Himalayan state of Uttarakhand, has ancient origins. According to Hindu mythology, it was built by the Pandavas, legendary heroes of the Mahabharata, who sought Lord Shiva’s forgiveness after the Kurukshetra War. The present temple was constructed in the 8th century A.D. by Adi Shankaracharya, and it is adjacent to the site of an earlier temple built by the Pandavas. The temple is believed to be more than 1200 years old.
